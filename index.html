The Executor

The Executor is a super star destroyer that was Darth Vader's flagship. However it did not see to fight many battles as it was destroyed during the second battle of the Death Star

What?

One of the largest and most powerful Imperial vessels ever created, the Executor was 19,000 meters long, eclipsing its escorting Imperial-class Star Destroyers, and boasted more than 5,000 turbolasers and ion cannons.

Brief History?

The Executor was built sometime between 2 BBY and 0 ABY and was destroyed on during the battle of the Second Death Star.

Price?

The Price of the Executor was estimated to be around 1,143,350,000 credits.
