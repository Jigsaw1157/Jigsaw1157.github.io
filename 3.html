The Eclipse

The Eclipse-class dreadnought, also known as the Eclipse-class Super Star Destroyer, was a class of Imperial Super Star Destroyer manufactured by Kuat Drive Yards

What?

The Eclipse-class vessels were among the largest dreadnought-scale ships/Super Star Destroyers ever to be fielded within Galactic history.

Brief History?

The Eclipse was constructed after the death of the emperor but the reborn version of him managed to survive and take command of it. Sadly he destroyed it with his force power, force storm.

Price?

The Eclipse also did not have an exact price but, we can assume since it was a super star destroyer that had a superlaser, that it costed somewhere near the first Death Star
